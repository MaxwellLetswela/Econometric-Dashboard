Overview

The SA FinTech Econometric Dashboard is a comprehensive sythetic macroeconomic tool designed for demonstration analysis for venture capital firms and corporate strategy divisions evaluating investments in South Africa's SMME FinTech sector. This dashboard highlights data-driven insights into market dynamics, valuation benchmarks, regulatory risks, and investment opportunities.



Purpose

To assist investment committees in making informed capital allocation decisions by analyzing:

Total Addressable Market (TAM) and growth trajectory

Competitive landscape and valuation multiples

Regulatory risk assessment and mitigation strategies

Macroeconomic correlations and sector opportunities

Comprehensive investment thesis development



Key Metrics Analyzed

Market Size: R2.8 Billion TAM (2024)

Growth Rate: 45% Year-over-Year (YoY)

Valuation Multiples: 9.4x Revenue (7.5x ARR) sector average

Funding Momentum: R220M quarterly (Q2 2024)

Regulatory Risk Score: 5.65/10 (Moderate-High)

Digital Adoption: 18% of target market
